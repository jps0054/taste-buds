
  Cavolfiore Alla Milanese
   (Fried Cauliflower)
Serves 6 people Preparation Time: 20 minutes Cooking Time: 30 minutes
    

INGREDIENTS

    - 
1 large cauliflower
    - 
2 eggs, beaten
1/4 cup oil    
    - 
3 tbsp butter
    - 
Bread crumbs
    - 
Salt and pepper to taste
    - 
    
    - 
    - 
    
    - 
    


INSTRUCTIONS
Remove outer leaves from cauliflower.
Bring water to a boil in a large saucepan 
 and add the cauliflower whole.
Cook for about 15 minutes.
Drain.
Cool and break into florets.
Dip cauliflower into flour and then into 
 the egg.
Coat with breadcrumbs.
In a large skillet, heat oil and butter 
 until very hot.
Add cauliflower, reduce heat and fry 
 for 10 minutes or until golden brown.
