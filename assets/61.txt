   Prosciutto Melone
  (HAM AND MELON)
 Serves 4 people Preparation Time: 5 minutes
    

INGREDIENTS

    - 
2 cantaloupes
    
12 thin slices of prosciutto
    - 
    
    


INSTRUCTIONS
Peel and slice the cantaloupes into
 12 slices.
Place three slices of cantaloupe per
 plate and lay a slice of prosciutto on 
 each.
