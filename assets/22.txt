 
     
   Chicken Broth
Makes 2 - 3 litres Preparation Time: 15 minutes Cooking Time: 2 1/2 hours
    

INGREDIENTS

    -
1 lb chicken
    
2 celery stalks
    
1 medium onion
    
2 large tomatos, chopped
    
1 large carrot
    -
5 litres water
    
Salt and pepper to taste
    
    
    -
    -
    
    
    


INSTRUCTIONS
 Cut chicken in half. In a large sauce
 pot place the chicken, celery, onion,
 carrot, salt and pepper along with the 
 water. Bring to a boil; reduce heat and 
 simmer for 2 hours.
 Remove the froth from the top with a 
 slotted spoon. Remove the chicken
 from the pot and reserve for use in 
 other dishes.
 Strain the broth through a fine sieve. 
 Cool and chill in the refrigerator.
 Keep refrigerated or freeze in small
 containers. Broth will keep in the
 refrigerator for up to 5 days.
