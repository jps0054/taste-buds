
   POLENTA
   (CORNMEAL)
Serves 6 people Preparation Time: 20 minutes Cooking Time: 1 HOUR 
In northern Italy, Polenta is a staple. It is served hot with butter as a side dish or for
breakfast. It can also be chilled, cut into squares and grilled or fried.
Note: This recipe is originally from the revised edition of Antonietta Terrignoâ€™s previous
 book entitled Italian Cooking, Classic Recipes, printed in May, 2005.
    

INGREDIENTS

    - 
4 (1 L) cups water
    
2 tbsp (30 mL) salt
1/2 cup (125 mL) olive oil   -
    
3 cups (750 mL) fine cornmeal
1/4 cup (60 mL) Parmesan cheese   -
    
    


INSTRUCTIONS
Bring the water to a boil and add the 
 salt and oil.
 Slowly whisk in the cornmeal and cook
 over low heat for 1 hour.
 Serve with tomato sauce and cod fish, 
 or zucchini, or sausage and sprinkle 
 with additional cheese.
