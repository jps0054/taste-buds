
  Potatoes
  (Side Dish)
 Preparation Time: 10 minutes Cooking Time: 45 minutes
   

INGREDIENTS

   - 
4 potatoes
   - 
1 medium onion, chopped
   
1 clove garlic
1 tbsp butter   -
2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil  - 
Salt and pepper to taste  - 
2 litres water   
   
   - 
   
   - 


INSTRUCTIONS
Boil water with a pinch of salt.
Peel and slice potatoes lengthwise
 to 1/2" thick.
 Boil in water for 5 minutes.
Drain water.
Add oil, butter, garlic and onion to 
 frying pan on medium heat for
 2 to 3 minutes.
Add potatoes, cover, and continue 
 at medium heat for 20 minutes.
Salt and pepper to taste and serve.
